### Postscript

I had the chance to join Unity's online reading group and share what I had learned about Shaders over the previous few months. I still did not have deep Shader experience at the time, so the focus was introductory: enough structure to help beginners get started.

It was also my first attempt at online teaching. The hardest part was avoiding wrong explanations. I spent a lot of time preparing the flow, checking for mistakes, and rehearsing. Looking back, there was still plenty to improve, but the process made Shader concepts much clearer to me.
