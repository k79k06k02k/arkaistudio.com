### Speaker | [Route 59 Games](http://www.route59games.com/) | Kevin Chen

_Kevin Chen founded Route 59 Games in 2015 to create narrative games that combine games, visual novels, and Japanese animation. Route 59's debut game, Necrobarista, uses a 3D visual novel style and takes place in a supernatural cafe where the dead can spend one final night with the living._

### Basic visual elements: perspective (2D ↔ 3D)

-   Humans are bad at rendering perspective accurately.
-   Machines are good at rendering perspective accurately.
-   Humans are good at recognizing faces.

Humans can still recognize faces even when perspective is distorted.
