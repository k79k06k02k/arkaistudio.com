**Tempo Fighting / Rhythm Action**
Developed with teammates during the 2016 VR Hackathon.

-   Targeted HTC Vive.
-   Built the underlying module framework.
-   Integrated the game flow.
-   Implemented enemy waves and entry AI.
-   Implemented character actions and states.
-   Built the beat system.
-   Produced and integrated music and sound effects.
