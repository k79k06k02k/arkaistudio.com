**Monster Fable / RPG match-3 development**

-   Built the onboarding system, including the underlying tutorial framework and related tools.
-   Implemented the art scene prefab and lightmap workflow.
-   Built asset splitting, dynamic downloads, and background download features.
-   Implemented AR features for characters and the main town with AR Foundation.
-   Developed systems for buildings, levels, character inventory and info, item crafting, story, missions, achievements, capsule draws, PvP, and mail.
